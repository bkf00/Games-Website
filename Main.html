Welcome to our website!

We are Cojocaru Bogdan and Baku Adrian and we wrote a short presentation of three games that we played and love. We are currently studying Electrical Engineering and Computer Science in English at the Transylvania University of Brasov and we decided to write our project about a subject that we both love, gaming. We hope that if you never played these three games before, this website will show you what amazing games they are and maybe, make you at least try them!

The three games that you will find on our website are Path of Exile, The Division 2 and The Witcher III: Wild Hunt, in their specific pages you will find a short description of the lore, the gameplay, a trailer and even some screenshots.

You can access them by clicking on their logos below or by clicking on their names in the header of the page!

If you have questions or maybe a request, you can contact us via the "Contact Us!" page, enjoy!
